## Business Overview

Northern Pathways is an immigration consulting firm led by a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC-IRB) and adjunct professor. The team, composed of immigrants, professionals, and educators, offers ethical, comprehensive, and personalized services for temporary residence, permanent economic immigration, family class, and citizenship applications in Canada.
